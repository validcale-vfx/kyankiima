Our History

Kyankima Women's Cooperative was founded in 2019 by 47 women in Mukono District who wanted to improve their livelihoods through farming and handcrafts. Starting with word-of-mouth and community radio, we have grown into a registered community-based organization.

Our Mission

To uplift women economically and socially by promoting sustainable agriculture, quality handcraft production, and community health education.
