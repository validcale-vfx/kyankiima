Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where is Kyankima Women's Cooperative located?

We are based in Mukono District, Uganda.

2. What products do you sell?

We sell fresh agricultural produce (maize, beans, groundnuts) and handmade crafts (baskets, mats, bags).

3. Do you deliver to Kampala?

Yes, we can arrange delivery upon request.

4. Are your products organic?

Most of our agricultural products are grown using natural methods.
